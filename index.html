IMPLEMENTAL

Implemental was founded to provide collaborative resources for the development, consultation, and implementation of AI-empowered, common-sense solutions for a select group of companies, in compelling vertical markets, that are well-positioned to leverage AI to maximize revenue and minimize costs through operational efficiency. The Company’s vision is to further leverage these foundational relationships to create a resource-rich platform capable of serving the needs of a wide variety of small medium and large businesses. From individual proprietorships to enterprise-level companies. Our Story

We are technicians, designers, developers, end-users and geeks. Problem solving is our passion and we love a challenge. We believe in building relationships, not just a client base. When you call you'll get to talk to a real person who is interested in your needs and wants to help you solve a problem. We are a provider of comprehensive, scalable, and secure application hosting, virtualization, and data center solutions. We service businesses across the United States while maintaining technical certifications and partner relationships with Microsoft, VMWare, Nimble Storage, Citrix Systems, Cisco, and Liebert.
